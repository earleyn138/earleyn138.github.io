As an undergraduate:

a long-term study of ULXs

I performed a comprehensive spectral modeling study of three ultraluminous X-ray sources in a nearby analog to the Milky Way, NGC 891. I analyzed the complete archival Chandra and XMM-Newton observations from 2000-2017, filling in gaps in the literature for previously known sources, and discovered a new transient source in the process which faded from view over the course of two months.
